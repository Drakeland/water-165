Energy Consumption, 2010

Created by Paul asdasdassd

Description:

Fifteen nations with GDP of greater than USD$1 trillion are shown. Their GDP is shown along the horizongal axis, and their energy consumption per capita is shown along the vertical axis. The radius of each circle is proportional to the nation's total energy consumption.
