Global Renewable Water Resources per Capita

1.2 billion people live in areas of physical water scarcity, a number projected to grow by 500 million by 2025, according to the UN Dept. of Economic and Social Affairs. The visualization above displays water scarcity levels of individual countries from 1962 to 2014 in five year increments. This information will allow users to view regions where water levels are most abundant and extremely scarce according to the color coded legend. In addition, users will be able to interact with the map with the given timeline, allowing users to choose a specific year. This map is measured in m3(H2O)/inhab/year.

Current year: 2014
