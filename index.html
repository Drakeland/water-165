Global Renewable Water Resources per Capita

m3(H2O)/inhab/year

Current year: 2014

Description

1.2 billion people live in areas of physical water scarcity, and that number is projected to grow by 500 million by 2025, according to the UN Dept. of Economic and Social Affairs. This problem is of great interest to society because universal water security is imperative to alleviating poverty from millions of people worldwide.
